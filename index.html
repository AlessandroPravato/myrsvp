Do you need a complete
RSVP & Event Planning
app for your social events?

Get it on

Weddings, parties, or recurrent occasions
we've got you covered.

1. Create as many events as you want

Every detail of your event is customizable. For each event you can choose different RSVP options that your invitees will see when they RSVP, and use specific features if needed: for example our tables manager, or guest registration.

Once your done, it's time to craft your event landing page!

2. Craft your event page

You can customize your event landing page and surprise your invitees with a creative invitation card!
Whoever has the link to this page can RSVP, check all your event details, and even navigate to the venue.

Now, you only need to invite someone!

See a preview

3. Fill up your invitees list

Before sending out your invites you need to have some invitees! You have 2 options:
a) You can create a private invitees list adding people manually or from different sources. For each invitee you can choose how to send your invitation which will have a personal message and an unique link
b) Or you can generate a public link to be shared anywhere you want. Anyone with the link can reply!

Once you're done, it is time to blast out your invitations or to publish your public link!

4. Manage your guest list

Sit back and watch your guest list grow as you start receiving all the RSVPs.

You can filter guests based on their attendance status, relationship or preference and you can edit their details and even their RSVP anytime, if needed.

What if you already know that someone is coming and you don't need to send out the invitation, or if a person is not really tech savy?
You can add attendees manually and RSVP on their behalf!

5. Arrange tables

Manage hundreds of tables with a finger.

You can sort out your guests with smart filtering, move guests across tables and even split groups!
On event day, you can see the live status of tables while registration progresses.

6. Guest registration

It's the big day! A guest brought extra people? Someone is missing?
No worries it's easy to manage last minute changes during registration and to rearrange seats accordingly.

And you don't have to do it alone! The guest registration list is synced across yours and your co-host devices, so you will work on the same list, in real time ;)

MyRsvp is packed with features to make you run your events smoothly,
from invitation to registration

Guest friendly

Your invitees can easily RSVP from your event landing page and we'll also send them an RSVP confirmation email once they do.
Invitees from your private invitees list are special and will be greeted with their name ;)
People RSVPing from a public link will also have to input their name and contact.

See a preview

Self-registration

If you required guest registration, guests will also receive a registration link with their RSVP confirmation email. With that, they can simply confirm their presence by tapping "I'm here" when they reach your venue!
This is synched with your registration list and you'll just need to check their phone for the confirmation.

You and your co-hosts will always be able to register guests manually, of course.

Keep track of events on the app!

Guests can be busy too! You can add an event to MyRSVP app using the code displayed on the event landing page.
Remember that for private events that code is personal for each invitee!
When you add an event to the app you'll be notified of any changes to the event details, and receive an RSVP reminder before the dealine ;)
