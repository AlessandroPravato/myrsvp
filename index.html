Are you a host? We've got you covered.


1. Create unlimited events

Have fun creating all the events that you want. Customize your special occasions, choose which tools you need, and fill in all the details.

Few clicks and you're ready to go!


2. Craft your invitation page

Surprise your invitees with a creative invitation card!

They'll be able to RSVP, check all your event details, and even navigate to the venue from your invitation page.

See a preview

3. Fill up your invitees list

Are you hosting a private event? Add people to your invitees list from different sources, categorise them, and choose how to send out your invitation.


4. Send out the invites

Now it is time to blast out your invites!

On private events each person will receive a personal message containing the link to your invitation page.


5. Manage your guest list

Sit back and watch your guest list grow as you start receiving all the RSVPs.

Hey, you don't need your grandma to RSVP if you already know she's coming...
Just add her as an attendee from this page.


6. Keep everything under control

Get all the info you need at a glance on the event overview page.

From here you can quickly access all your management tools such as table seating, registration, and angpow book.

MyRsvp is packed with features to make you run your events smoothly, from invitation to registration.

Synced registration
Enjoy fast and smooth registration synced among all co-host.

A guest brought extra people? No problem: It's easy to add them during registration and even easier to rearrange seats accordingly.

Remember to keep an eye on the % of arrived guests.
Table management
Manage hundreds of tables with a finger. You can sort out your guests with smart filtering, move guests across tables and see the live status of tables on event day while registration progresses.
Custom RSVP questions
Are you providing additional services to your guests like baby chairs or halal menu?

Great! your invitees will be able to indicate how many people need these services when they RSVP from your invitation page.
Invitation card editor
Our selection of card templates is not enough for you?

Upload your own design or get full creative control with a powerful card editor.
Unlimited co-hosts
Share the workload with your loved ones.

Co-hosts can manage everything from their phones but they won't be able to modify the event details or purchase pro features on your behalf.
Angpow book
Do you need to keep track of all those angpows?

Divide all the envelops among your co-hosts so everyone can quickly record guests' angpows amounts on a shared list!
Smart filters
You will be dealing with long lists of names so it is important that you find who you're looking for quickly.

Filter by alphabet, attendance status, RSVP preferences, and relationship.

Are you looking for attending vegetarian friends? Filter, done.
Export everything to pdf
Export your guest list, seating arrangements and past event reports to extremely detailed pdf files.

That way you can print them out or share them with anyone.

What about your guests? Same app!


Keep track of all your events

Busy being a guest? Keep all the events you've been invited to in one place.

If you already RSVP to an event before installing the app you can always add it to your list using the event code later on.


RSVP and event info

Check all the event info and RSVP to the invitation at any time.

You'll be notified of any changes whenever the host updates something.


Self-Registration!

If the host provides self-registration you can skip completely the queue and just tap "I'm here" when you reach the venue.

You can also check your table number after registering on events with arranged seating.
